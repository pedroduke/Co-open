The Beauty of Reaction

The Beauty of Reaction it's a photographic project which shows the natural reaction between diesel, rain and sun.

 

The project was developed taking a keen eye and appreciate the sun reflecting in objects found at  the streets of the busy city of   London, UK while exploring what the city have to offer and at the end came across with this simple and beautiful phenomenon.

Here Was Life

Here was Life it's a photographic project about the old Metalurgica factory in Castelo Branco, Portugal

 

"Fragmentos de espacos e tempos, restos de epocas e locais onde apenas habitam memorias e fantasmas. Vestigios de coisas sobre as quais o tempo, os elementos, a natureza, e a propria accao humana modificaram e modificam. Com o tempo tudo deixa de ser, transformando-se eventualmente numa outra coisa.

Lugares que deixaram de fazer sentido, de serem necessarios, de estar na moda. Lugares esquecidos, obsoletos, inospitos, vazios."

Dark Shapes // Exodus

Dark Shapes is an adiovisual project which connect video mapping and live performance music. A video teaser shows a small part of the live performance.

 

Exodus it's one of the projects developed in Festival Aldeias Artisticas, Portugal in which describes a passage from the life of the city to the life on an small village.
